# Nigeria to Germany — Student Visa

- Proof of funds via a blocked account (~€11,000) or sponsorship letter with notarization and income evidence.
- Academic Evaluation Center (APS) certificates are mandatory for many applicants; book early to avoid delays.
- Health insurance (public or recognized private) should start from arrival date; include confirmation letters.
- Universities often require enrollment certificates; carry admission letter and fee payment proof to the visa interview.
